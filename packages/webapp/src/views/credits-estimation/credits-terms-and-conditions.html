Introduction

The statutory biodiversity credit scheme is run by Natural England on behalf of the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (Defra) as part of the Environment Act 2021.

The scheme enables developers or landowners to purchase credits if they are unable to meet their BNG (Biodiversity Net Gain) obligation. This could be either through on-site, off-site on other landholdings, or by purchasing biodiversity units.

The credits will go towards achieving their BNG obligation.

Not transferable

The issued Statutory Biodiversity Credits may not be sold, transferred or moved to any third party (whether a person, company or other organisation).

Liability

It is your responsibility to ensure you have purchased the correct number, and tier, of Statutory Biodiversity Credits to meet your BNG obligation.
