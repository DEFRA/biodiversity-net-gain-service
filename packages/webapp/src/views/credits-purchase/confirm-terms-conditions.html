Introduction

The statutory biodiversity credit scheme is run by Natural England on behalf of the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (Defra) as part of the Environment Act 2021.

Privacy notice

Our privacy notice sets out how we use the personal data.

Your responsibilities

It is your responsibility to ensure:

  • you have purchased the correct number, and tier, of statutory biodiversity credits to meet your BNG obligation for development under the Planning Permission referenced in your application

Customer due diligence

All applications to purchase statutory biodiversity credits are subject to customer due diligence (anti-money laundering) checks. They can only be purchased by applicants who pass those checks.

Purchase terms

You have no obligation to Natural England to buy the statutory biodiversity credits in this service. The terms and conditions for purchase of statutory biodiversity credits will apply if you proceed.
