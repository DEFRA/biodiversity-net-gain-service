How to discharge your BNG condition

If your details are registered, you will need to add them to your Biodiversity Net Gain Plan.

By law you will then need to submit this to your local planning authority so they can discharge your BNG planning condition.

Make a note of your reference number. This will be used by Natural England to identify your submission, if they have any questions about what you've asked to be recorded on the Public Biodiversity Gain Register.
