You will need written consent from the landowner of the biodiversity gain site. This will need to include:

  • a written statement confirming the landowner's consent
  • the landowner's signature
  • the date consent was agreed (this must be uploaded within 3 months of it being written)

You could be prosecuted or have to pay a fine if you record off-site gain without consent.
